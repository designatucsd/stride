Design-Centric Career Fair

January 2020

Stride connects top design students to career opportunities in the fields of human-centered design, human factors, user research, user experience, interaction design, UI design, visual design, content strategy, product management, product development, brand design, product design, UX engineering, web development and much more.

Made for Designers

The traditional career fair format isn’t catered toward students seeking design roles. Designers need a medium to showcase their work and experience. At Stride, students have the opportunity to secure individual time with recruiters to present their projects and portfolios.

Companies

Our corporate partners represent multiple industries and diverse product lines and services. Come to Stride to learn more about opportunities at a wide range of organizations.
